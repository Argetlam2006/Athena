# Product Overview

Athena is an AI-powered Football Decision Intelligence Platform that enables football analysts, sporting directors and recruitment teams to transform football data into explainable insights and actionable decisions.

Athena combines structured football data, statistical analysis, machine learning, interactive visualizations and AI-assisted reasoning into a unified analytical workflow.

Version 1 focuses on three intelligence modules:

- Player Intelligence
- Team Intelligence
- Recruitment Intelligence

These modules are unified through a conversational AI assistant capable of explaining insights generated by Athena's analytical engine.

---

# Problem Statement

Football organizations increasingly rely on data when evaluating players, teams and recruitment opportunities.

However, existing tools typically fall into one of three categories:

- Statistical dashboards that require manual interpretation.
- Scouting platforms that prioritize observation over quantitative analysis.
- AI assistants that generate responses without structured analytical grounding.

This creates several challenges.

- Analysts spend significant time navigating multiple tools.
- Insights remain fragmented across different data sources.
- Recommendations often lack transparency.
- Decision-makers struggle to connect statistics with practical football decisions.

Athena addresses these challenges by combining structured analytics, explainable AI and interactive exploration into a single decision-support platform.

---

# Product Goal

Build a football intelligence platform capable of helping users answer practical football questions through evidence-based analytics.

Examples include

- Which players best match our tactical requirements?
- What type of player is this?
- How does this team play?
- Which transfer represents the highest value?
- Why does Athena recommend one player over another?
